**Santorini, Greece Travel Budget Breakdown and Booking Tips Document**

**Summary:**

- **Accommodation:** Expect to pay around $150-$200 per night for a hotel or Airbnb. Consider budget-friendly options like hostels or guesthouses in Fira town.
- **Transportation:** Use public transportation, such as buses, to get around the island. Taxi rides can be expensive, and parking fees can add up.
- **Food and drink:** Eat at local tavernas or street food stalls for a more authentic experience. Try the local cuisine, including Greek salad, souvlaki, and fresh seafood.
- **Activities and entrance fees:** Plan your activities and entrance fees in advance to avoid any last-minute expenses.
- **Total budget:** Expect to pay around $600-$800 for a budget-friendly option, $800-$1,200 for a mid-range option, and $1,500-$2,500 for a luxury option.
